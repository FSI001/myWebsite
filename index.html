Set up in 2009, DPS STS School Dhaka has become one of the most respected and academically
acclaimed schools in Bangladesh. The school follows the Cambridge International curriculum from
Primary to Grade 12. It is spread over two campuses (2.21 acres), one dedicated to junior sections
and the other housing the senior sections.
